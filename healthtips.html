Healthy Eating Tips

1. Include a Variety of Foods

Eat a balanced diet with a variety of foods to ensure you're getting a range of nutrients.

2. Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water throughout the day to stay hydrated. Aim for at least 8 cups daily.

3. Choose Whole Grains

Opt for whole grains over refined grains to increase your fiber intake and improve digestion.

4. Eat Plenty of Fruits and Vegetables

Include a variety of colorful fruits and vegetables in your meals for essential vitamins and minerals.

5. Limit Added Sugars and Salt

Reduce your intake of foods high in added sugars and salt to promote better health.

6. Practice Portion Control

Be mindful of portion sizes to help manage your weight and avoid overeating.

7. Plan Your Meals

Plan your meals ahead of time to make healthier choices and avoid impulsive eating.

8. Enjoy Your Meals

Take time to enjoy your food. Eating slowly can help you recognize when you're full.

Recipe Suggestions

Quinoa Salad

A refreshing mix of quinoa, vegetables, and dressing.

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
  1. Rinse the quinoa under cold water.
  2. In a pot, bring water to a boil, add quinoa, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Fluff with a fork and let it cool.
  4. In a bowl, combine quinoa, vegetables, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  5. Toss well and serve chilled.

Grilled Chicken & Veggies

A simple, healthy meal with protein and fiber.

Ingredients:
  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (zucchini, bell peppers, etc.)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
  1. Preheat grill to medium heat.
  2. Rub chicken with olive oil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Grill chicken for 6-7 minutes per side until cooked through.
  4. Grill vegetables until tender, about 5-7 minutes.
  5. Serve chicken with grilled veggies on the side.

Healthy Green Smoothie

A nutritious smoothie packed with fruits and greens.

Ingredients:
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy immediately.

Chickpea Curry

A hearty and filling dish with spices and chickpeas.

Ingredients:
  • 2 cans chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cilantro for garnish
Instructions:
  1. In a pot, sauté onion and garlic until soft.
  2. Add curry powder and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add chickpeas, tomatoes, and coconut milk.
  4. Simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, garnish with cilantro, and serve.

Zucchini Noodles with Pesto

A low-carb alternative to pasta, topped with pesto.

Ingredients:
  • 2 medium zucchinis, spiralized
  • 1/4 cup pesto
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add zucchini noodles and cook for 2-3 minutes until tender.
  3. Stir in pesto and cherry tomatoes, cooking for another minute.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, then serve warm.
